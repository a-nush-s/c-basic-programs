this program will create a binary search tree. 
you can input, search for, or delete a value by including "i", "s", or "d" before it. 
the program will print if the value was inserted, present or absent, or success or fail, depending on your command. 
input files should be formatted as follows (view in code)
i 10
i 4
i 3
s 4
s 5
i 12
d 4
d 5
